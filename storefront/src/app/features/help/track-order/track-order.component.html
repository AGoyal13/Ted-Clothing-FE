How Tracking Works

  1. 1
    Order Confirmed
    You receive an order confirmation email with your order ID within a few minutes of purchase.
  2. 2
    Order Dispatched
    Once your order is packed and handed to our logistics partner, you receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. This typically happens within 1–2 business days of order placement.
  3. 3
    In Transit
    Use the tracking link to follow your shipment in real time. Standard delivery within India takes 4–7 business days from dispatch.
  4. 4
    Out for Delivery
    You'll receive a notification on the morning of delivery. Ensure someone is available at the delivery address to accept the package.
  5. 5
    Delivered
    Once delivered, your order status in My Account updates automatically. Inspect your package immediately and report any issues within 48 hours.

Ordered as a Guest?

Your tracking link is included in the dispatch confirmation email sent to your registered email address. Search your inbox for an email from noreply@tedclothing.in with subject line "Your order is on its way."

If you can't locate the email, check your spam folder or contact us at hello@tedclothing.in with your order ID and we'll resend the tracking details.

Delivery Partners

Ted Clothing ships with leading logistics providers across India. Depending on your pin code, your order may be handled by Delhivery, BlueDart, Xpressbees, or Ecom Express.

Courier assignment is automatic and cannot be changed after dispatch.

Order Delayed or Stuck?

If your tracking shows no movement for more than 3 business days, or if the estimated delivery date has passed, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@tedclothing.in
  • WhatsApp: +91 99999 99999

Please include your order ID and the last tracking status for faster resolution. We aim to respond within 24 business hours.
