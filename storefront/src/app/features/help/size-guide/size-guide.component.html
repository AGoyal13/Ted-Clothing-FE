Size Guide

All measurements are in inches unless stated otherwise. When in doubt, size up.

How to Measure

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 8 inches below the waist.

Shoulder

Measure from the outer edge of one shoulder seam to the other across the back.

Fit Notes

  • True to size — Ted Clothing pieces are designed to fit as labelled. If you prefer a more relaxed silhouette, size up by one.
  • Linen & cotton blends may shrink slightly after the first wash. We recommend cold wash to retain original dimensions.
  • Structured pieces (blazers, trousers) are cut with a slight ease; shoulder fit is the primary guide.
  • Still unsure? Email us at hello@tedclothing.in with your measurements and the product name — we'll recommend the right size.
