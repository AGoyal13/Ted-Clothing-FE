3. Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible, the item must meet all of the following conditions at the time of pickup:

  • Unworn & unwashed — not worn, washed, or altered in any way.
  • Original tags intact — all brand tags and labels attached as received.
  • Original packaging — in the original Ted Clothing poly-bag or box.
  • No damage by customer — not torn, stained, perfumed, or damaged after delivery.
  • Correct item — the item matches the order it was placed against.

Doorstep quality check: Our delivery partner performs an inspection at pickup. Items that fail the quality check will be refused and returned to you — no @switch (returnMode()) { @case ('return') { refund } @case ('exchange') { exchange } @case ('both') { refund or exchange } } will be processed for refused items.

4. Non-Eligible Items

The following are not eligible under any circumstances:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
  • Personalized or made-to-order items
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at time of purchase
  • Free gifts or promotional items
  • Items used, washed, or damaged after delivery
