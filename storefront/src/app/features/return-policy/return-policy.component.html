3. Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a return, the item must meet all of the following conditions at the time of pickup:

  • Unworn & unwashed — the product has not been worn, washed, or altered in any way.
  • Original tags intact — all brand tags and labels must be attached as received.
  • Original packaging — returned in the original Ted Clothing poly-bag or box.
  • No damage by customer — not torn, stained, perfumed, or otherwise damaged after delivery.
  • Correct item — the item being returned matches the order it was placed against.

Items that fail inspection at the time of pickup will be returned to you and no refund will be processed.

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following categories are not eligible for return under any circumstances:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Personalized or made-to-order items
  • Items explicitly marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page at the time of purchase
  • Free gifts or promotional items included with an order
  • Items that have been used, washed, or damaged after delivery

5. How to Initiate a Return

Returns can only be requested through your Ted Clothing account — we do not accept walk-in or self-shipped returns.

  1. 1
    Log in to your account at tedclothing.com and go to My Account → Orders.
  2. 2
    Find the delivered order and click Request Return. This option is visible only within the {{ returnWindowDays() > 0 ? returnWindowDays() + '-day' : '' }} return window.
  3. 3
    Our logistics partner will contact you to schedule a pickup at your delivery address within 2–3 business days.
  4. 4
    Keep the item packed in its original packaging with all tags intact. Hand it over to the delivery agent.
  5. 5
    Once the item is received and inspected at our warehouse, your refund will be initiated within 2 business days.

7. Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Items

If you receive an item that is damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at hello@tedclothing.in with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photographs of the item and the packaging
  • A brief description of the issue

Verified cases will be resolved with either a full replacement (subject to stock availability) or a complete refund — at no additional cost to you.
